APPLE
Apples are crisp, juicy fruits available in a variety of colors and flavors, known for their high fiber content and versatility in both sweet and savory dishes.

MANGO
Mangoes are tropical fruits with a vibrant orange flesh, offering a sweet and slightly tangy flavor, and are packed with vitamins A and C.
ORANGE
Oranges are citrus fruits with a tangy, refreshing taste, high in vitamin C and often enjoyed fresh or as juice to boost immunity and hydration.
